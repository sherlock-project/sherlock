    About My Library
    "My library" contains books I have read in the past, or own and intend to read. Very few of those are still physically in my possession since I periodically get read of paper books I own. Sometimes I will come across a book I read a long time ago and add it to my library, but mostly I add them as I acquire or read them.
    My "To read" collection contains books I own and actually intend to read (as opposed to free e-books I downloaded just in case). This list has grown too long lately and I'm trying to reduce its size.
